# Lead quality

## Definition
The operating measure used to decide whether acquisition output is acceptable
for sales follow-up.

## Is not
Lead quality is not the raw lead count.

## Identity criteria
The metric is present when the receiving sales role can use it to accept or
reject a lead handoff.

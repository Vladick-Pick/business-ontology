# CRM

## Definition
The operating tool where acquisition and sales record lead-stage facts.

## Is not
The CRM is not the lead-quality scoring convention.

## Identity criteria
A system is the CRM when revenue operations treats it as the authoritative
place to inspect current lead-stage records.

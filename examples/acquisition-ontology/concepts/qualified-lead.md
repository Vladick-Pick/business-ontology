# Qualified lead

## Definition
A lead that acquisition has prepared enough for sales to accept into work.

## Is not
A raw inbound contact without the minimum profile and intent evidence.

## Identity criteria
The lead has the required fields, enough intent evidence, and a receiving
sales owner.
